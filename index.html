Hello, I'm Venkatesh

I'm a Gameplay Programmer at Kartoffel Technology, based in Tamil Nadu, India, with nearly two years of production game development experience. I specialize in building the systems that make games actually feel good — AI behavior, character controllers, gameplay architecture, and performance optimization that keeps the frame rate stable when things get chaotic.

My deepest work has been in enemy AI, gameplay systems, and swarm behavior experimentation in Unreal Engine 5. I've explored techniques for handling large groups of AI efficiently while maintaining responsive gameplay and system architecture.

I'm looking for opportunities to work with teams that value technical excellence, creative problem-solving, and gameplay innovation. I want to build systems that are fun, scalable, and memorable, creating experiences players enjoy long after they've finished the game.
