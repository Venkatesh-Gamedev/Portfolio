  • DEATH POINT


    A gripping game where players control a glowing sphere navigating a haunted graveyard. The goal is to collect cursed skulls to unlock the path to victory. With deadly traps and spectral enemies at every turn, only those who gather enough skulls can escape the eerie graveyard. Death Point offers a challenging and atmospheric experience that keeps players on edge.

  • DRONE BLITZ


    Drone Blitz is a high-intensity, single-level FPS set in a sprawling, abandoned city. Players must shoot down a swarm of hostile drones within a strict time limit, utilizing limited ammo and tactical positioning. The urban battlefield is littered with crumbling buildings, abandoned vehicles, and overgrown streets, demanding quick reflexes and sharp aim to survive. As each drone is destroyed, the action escalates, and the clock inches closer to zero. Easy to pick up but thrilling to master, Drone Blitz delivers an adrenaline-packed challenge for fans of fast-paced action.

  • SIDESCROLLER


    A vibrant and energetic 2D platformer where players navigate forward and backward to collect coins while overcoming colorful obstacles. With its dynamic levels, engaging gameplay, and bright, immersive design, SideScroller delivers a fun-filled experience perfect for players of all ages.
